Day 2/100 Cycling: 24km Kikuyu-Kamangu-Ondiri Loop

Aug 25, 2021
1 min read
  • Date/Time: 24th August 2021 16:34
  • Distance: 24.83km
  • Time: 02:23:52
  • Average Speed: 10.36km/h

This ride was initially supposed to take me to Mai Mahiu junction in Ndeiya but I cut it short due to time constraints. The loop starts in Kikuyu on the Southern Bypass until the Dagoretti Road Interchange where I took Dagoretti Road all the way to Kamangu Area. Kamangu area adjacent to Maasai plain is quite scenic with undulating hills and valleys. It is a famous rock climbing venue.

I noticed a significant decent from Kikuyu since the bike was going downhill most of the time. I am sure if I am to hit this loop going in reverse, It will be quite tough.
